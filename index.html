Things I will cook for you if I like you

Dumplings, like (my) Grandma made

No shortcuts here. Everything is done painstakingly by hand, including the soft dough. This labor of love takes an army - gather a dozen of your friends, and make a party out of this aseembly line meal. Go heavy on the innards, and light on the soy sauce.

Miso Claypot Chicken (No Claypot)

My new favorite one-pot-wonder. Throw the sauce in a bowl, slather it on the chicken, toss it over some rice, slam the lid, and then let the rice cooker do all the hard work. When you hear the rice cooker jingle, your appetite will tingle!

Lion's Head

Asian meatballs on noodles. It's like working with playdough, that's deliciously edible. Make em fat, make em small, make em however you like. Lay them on top of your (Asian) carb of choice.
